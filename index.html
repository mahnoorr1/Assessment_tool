Results

Strategy is relooked and refreshed often in our organization (at least once or twice a year)?

Strategy is formulated through a robust data-driven approach (looking at market data, industry data, internal performance data, benchmarks, etc.)

When strategy is developed, we effectively capture the voice of our key stakeholders (e.g. leadership, customers, government, employees, etc.)

Our strategy is effectively articulated into clear Objectives/goals, KPIs/Targets, and Strategic Actions?

Our long term strategic plan is broken down into a more shorter term business plans for the organization?

Other key management tools and plans in the organization are aligned with the strategy (Financial budget, manpower plan, training plan risk mgmt, etc.)

Our strategy is cascaded from corporate levels to lower levels of the organization (divisions, departments, etc.) and eventually cascaded to the individual?

Our operating model (people, process, structure, technology) is aligned to the strategy? , and in enough places to significantly influence overall company results

Our incentive and bonus scheme is directly connected to the achievement of strategic goals & objectives?

A strategy communication plan is in place, ensuring regular communication of the company's strategy to the extent that a majority of employees understand it?

Our values, organizational behaviors, and initiatives related to organization culture are tightly linked to the strategy?

Leadership is bought in to the strategy and role model the change needed to get the strategy executed?

We have a dedicated office/department managing the strategy lifecycle and its execution?

Corporate and departmental strategies are reviewed and actioned on a periodic basis (monthly or quarterly)?

Our reporting architecture is fully automated through a strategy management or performance management system?
